Welcome to Kartick's Homepage



                        
                                                                                                                 



Mr. Kartick Chandra Mondal received his Bachelor of Technology degree in Information Technology from Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College, Jalpaiguri, West Bengal, India in 2006. In 2009, he has completed his Master of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from University of Kalyani, Nadia, West Bengal, India. Presently, he is doing his Ph. D. from University of Nice Sophia-Antilopis in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering from November 2009. He is working as a doctoral researcher in the Laboratory of I3S (Laboratoire d'Informatique, Signaux et Systemes de Sophia-Antipolis, I3S-UMR6070-UNS CNRS) under Pole GLC (Genie du Logiciel et de la Connaissance). He is the recipient of West Bengal state Governmental Scholarship (for carrying out his bachelor's study from 2002-2006), Indian National Fellowship (for carrying out his master's study from 2007-2009) and Doctoral Fellowship from European Union (for completing his Ph.D. from 2009-2012).

His research interest includes Evolutionary Computing, Data Mining, Bio-informatics, Multi-Objective Optimization.
